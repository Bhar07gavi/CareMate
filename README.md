**CareMate** is a user-friendly desktop application designed to support elderly individuals and their caregivers by addressing daily challenges such as medication management, safety monitoring, health tracking, and emotional well-being.  
Built using **Python and Tkinter**, CareMate provides a simple, intuitive, and non-intrusive experience for users with minimal technical skills.

---

## üöÄ Features

- **Daily Reminders**  
  Voice and popup notifications for medications and appointments, triggered using a CSV-based schedule.

- **Safety Monitoring**  
  Alerts for missed check-ins or safety concerns displayed in a clear tabular format.

- **Health Monitoring**  
  Visual tracking of vitals such as blood pressure, glucose levels, and heart rate to identify abnormal trends early.

- **Motivational Quotes**  
  Categorized motivational quotes with optional voice narration to boost morale and reduce loneliness.

- **Exercise Suggestions**  
  Personalized activity recommendations to encourage physical movement and healthy habits.

- **Emergency & Contact Access**  
  One-click access to emergency assistance or family contacts for immediate support.

- **Voice Assistance & Fullscreen UI**  
  Easy navigation with voice support and fullscreen layout for improved accessibility.

---

## üß† Methodology

CareMate follows a **user-centric and problem-driven design approach**, focusing on simplicity, accessibility, and reliability.

- Developed using **Python with Tkinter** for a fullscreen, intuitive GUI
- Uses **CSV files** for lightweight, editable data storage
- Integrated **pyttsx3** for voice interaction and accessibility
- Designed for **offline functionality**, reducing dependency on internet connectivity
- UI uses **soft pastel themes**, large buttons, and clutter-free layouts suitable for elderly users
